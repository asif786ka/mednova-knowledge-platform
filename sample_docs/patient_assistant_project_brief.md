# Project Brief — Patient Assistant Platform

**Client:** St. Aldwyn Hospital
**Project:** Patient Assistant Platform

## Summary
The Patient Assistant Platform is an AI-powered patient support system that provides
voice-based digital assistance to patients. It uses AI automation to triage patient queries,
book appointments, and answer common questions.

## Technologies connected to the patient assistant platform
The Patient Assistant Platform uses **Speech-to-Text** (Whisper) and **Text-to-Speech** for its
voice interface, **LangChain** for dialogue orchestration, and **Twilio** for telephony. It relies
on **AI automation** for query triage.

## Requirements
- The platform **requires voice-based AI** with speech-to-text and text-to-speech.
- Multilingual voice support is required for English and Welsh.
- Responses must be HIPAA-compliant and patient data must remain encrypted.

## Deployment
The Patient Assistant Platform is deployed on **Microsoft Azure**.
