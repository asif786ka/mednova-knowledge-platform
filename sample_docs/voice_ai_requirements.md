# Voice AI Requirements

## Scope
This document defines voice-based AI requirements across MedNova projects.

The Patient Assistant Platform requires voice-based AI. A voice assistant requires Speech-to-Text
to transcribe patient audio and Text-to-Speech to synthesise spoken replies.

## Requirements
- The voice assistant requires Speech-to-Text (Whisper) with < 300 ms streaming latency.
- The voice assistant requires Text-to-Speech with natural prosody.
- Voice interactions must be logged and traceable for clinical audit.
- Voice-based AI must support English and Welsh.

## Related services
Voice-based AI is related to patient engagement and to the Patient Assistant Platform's triage
service.
